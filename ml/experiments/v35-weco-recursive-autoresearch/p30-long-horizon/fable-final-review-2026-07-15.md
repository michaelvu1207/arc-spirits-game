# Final pre-authorization review: V35 P30 long-horizon campaign

**Scope reviewed:** `plan.md`; `confirmation-gates.proposed.json`; `ml/analyze_v35_p30_long_horizon.py` (all 4,093 lines); `ml/audit_v32_generation.py`; `ml/v35_p30_analysis_review.py`; `ml/v35_p30_authorized_execution.py` (all 2,447 lines); `ml/run_v35_p30_campaign.py` (all 2,072 lines); `scripts/run-v35-p30-root.sh`.

## Prior findings — verification per dimension

**1. Quantitative confirmation gates — addressed.** `confirmation-gates.proposed.json` is a complete, conjunctive, machine-readable contract (`promotionEligible: false`, frozen-before-P30-outcomes) whose every threshold matches plan §Phase 5. I independently verified the win-count/Wilson-bound consistency: 6,631/8,192 gives a one-sided Wilson lower bound of ≈0.8000 at z = 2.128 (Bonferroni α = 0.05/3), 7,431/8,192 gives ≈0.9000; the private-seed counts 3,337 and 3,732 of 4,096 and the 751/1,024 slice floor are likewise exact at their stated z values. The protocol pins the file by SHA-256 and the analyzer fails closed if it is missing or hash-invalid.

**2. Divergence classification — addressed.** Recovery is restricted to objectively provable `PRE_CHILD` (forbidden once `Popen` is entered; supervisor-created streams are deleted and any surviving output makes recovery ambiguous and terminal) and `RECEIPT_ONLY` (its sealing path contains no command or `Popen` at all). Consumed-token-without-draft, invalid unsigned receipts, second attempt markers, and nested recovery all terminally close the campaign. Deterministic replay divergence has no recovery class: the guardian pair receipt admits only `EXACT_PER_GAME_MATCH`/`EXACT_REPLAY_HASH_MATCH`, and the analyzer independently recompares full per-game rows and replay hashes.

**3. Realized-dose telemetry — addressed.** `validate_reach30_credit` in the audit core now requires applied-row counts and realized dose per round band (1-8, 9-18, 19-30), checks dose = applied × frozen coefficient per band, that band sums equal totals, that control-zero applied exactly zero, and that enabled arms applied more than zero. These quantities are sealed in the signed audit and are not read by the analyzer's selection logic, matching the "cannot affect scheduling or selection" constraint.

**4. Duration/storage go-no-go — addressed.** `_rolling_budget_guard` runs before every scheduled action after the preflight: it recomputes retained bytes and runtime from the larger of the signed preflight estimate and the running maximum across signed receipts, requires free-plus-reserved ≥ 6 GiB floor + 512 MiB evaluation reserve + 25%-buffered projected remaining bytes, maintains a `posix_fallocate` reservation (flock, fsync, `st_blocks` non-sparse check) covering all actions after the next one, releases exactly one action's allocation per step, returns the reservation to zero only after all 54 pair receipts, and stops non-discretionarily if `accumulated + duration_max × remaining` exceeds the frozen 1,209,600-second ceiling. The guardian preflight receipt independently pins the same floor, reserve, and ceiling.

**5. Local-only review provenance — addressed.** Unsigned review receipts are explicitly rejected (`validate_analysis_review_draft` raises unconditionally). The receipt must be signed by the locally custodied review-attester key; the analyzer and scheduler both verify the signature, the `O_EXCL` attempt reservation consumed before the review, the pinned Claude executable (path, bytes, version, cross-checked against the protocol's `reviewRuntime`), the default-deny `sandbox-exec` profile with capsule-only reads/writes, `Read` as the sole tool, empty stderr, the exact `VERDICT: ACCEPT` sentinel, and chronology (reservation → review start → finish, after draft and manifest issuance). An attempt marker without a receipt yields a closed `analysis-review-attempt-consumed` lane with no retry path.

**6. One-shot launch/signing boundaries — addressed.** Every child launch requires a launch permit validated before token consumption or `Popen`, bound to the exact request, authorization, protocol, source contract, and the live executor process identity (pid/startTicks/uid/gid/host/bootId), so replay or a stale permit fails. The executor validates the full 537-file source closure internally, and its direct CLI is disabled. fsync-backed seal-attempt markers are committed before any private key is touched; the key arrives only as a sealed memfd descriptor at signing time; a second seal attempt of the same action is terminal. The analysis child additionally proves its identity via a fully sealed anonymous memfd capability (uniqueness-of-descriptor checked), fresh PID/user/network namespaces distinct from the supervisor's, executable-hash equality with the authorization, and a ≤5-minute consumed→intent→evidence window.

**7. Exact-stack determinism — addressed.** The Phase 0 bitwise primary/replay smoke remains a signed acceptance gate, the per-generation environment manifest records Python/Torch/CUDA/cuDNN versions and the determinism environment (`PYTHONHASHSEED=0`, `CUBLAS_WORKSPACE_CONFIG=:4096:8`, pinned GPU UUID), and the campaign structurally enforces the claim end-to-end: replay uses a distinct token, socket, and attempt, its authorization consumes the primary receipt as predecessor, and any output divergence fails both the guardian pair receipt and the analyzer's independent recomparison with no recovery path.
