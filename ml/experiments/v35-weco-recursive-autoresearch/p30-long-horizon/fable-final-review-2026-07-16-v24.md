## Named review areas

**1. Quantitative confirmation gates — addressed.** `confirmation-gates.proposed.json` freezes every Phase-5 number the plan states, exactly: 8,192 fresh public games with 6,631/7,431 minimum wins and the 100,000-draw paired-bootstrap `+0.05` post-round-15 VP floor at Bonferroni-corrected confidence; 3,337/3,732 private wins with 1,024-game late-state slice floors; Elo lower-95 `+50` overall, nonnegative per format, seat spread ≤ 0.02; exploitability `+0.03`/`-0.01`/`0.40`/`-0.02`; zero-tolerance fairness and systems counters; latency ceilings (100 ms, 1.10×, 60 s, 1.25×, 1.10×); 28/40 Michael games with ties as non-wins; explicit conjunctive decision rule. The screen-side inference (18-pair exact sign-flip with 262,144 enumerations, Holm correction, win-effect and replicate-consistency gates, deterministic tie-break, conservative tie tolerance) is implemented and regression-tested; the analyzer imports the power calibrator's `build` for recomputation against the hash-bound artifact registered in its source paths.

**2. Divergence classification — addressed.** `PRE_CHILD`/`RECEIPT_ONLY` are the only recovery classes, cross-checked against the frozen protocol's `recoveryPolicy.eligibleKinds`; only generation/evaluation kinds are recoverable. A consumed token without a canonical draft, an invalid draft that entered `Popen`, unsealed state on a non-recoverable kind, or a second attempt (`attemptOrdinal != 1`, `secondRecoveryForbidden`, nested recovery-of-recovery) all terminate the instance. A CUDA primary/replay mismatch raises inside the preflight child and cannot be classified as recoverable infrastructure. Training-quality failure cannot be relabelled: non-finite tensors fail the audit, while weak-but-valid checkpoints pass through unchanged.

**3. Realized-dose telemetry — addressed.** `validate_reach30_credit` in the trusted core audit parses and enforces applied reach-30 rows and realized coefficient dose overall and in the exact 1-8 / 9-18 / 19-30 bands, requires band sums to reconcile with the total dose to 1e-9, requires per-band dose to equal applied-rows × the frozen schedule coefficient, forbids any applied credit in `control-zero`, and requires nonzero application in treatment arms. The telemetry is sealed into every generation audit in the chain and re-surfaced by the analyzer only from verified receipts (`_verifiedReach30DoseTelemetry`), with tests at both layers.

**4. Duration/storage go-no-go — addressed.** The guardian preflight receipt is rejected unless `projectedCampaignComputeSeconds` ≤ the protocol's frozen 14-day ceiling and `projectedFinalFreeBytes` ≥ the 6 GiB floor, with the 512 MiB evaluation reserve and 25% safety factor bound as exact fields. The `posix_fallocate` reservation verifies physical allocation (`st_blocks*512 ≥ size`), detects sparseness, shrinks one action at a time under an exclusive lock, and live headroom is re-proven on every scheduler pass, not just once. These are non-discretionary `ValueError` paths.

**5. Local-only review provenance — addressed.** `validate_gate_review_receipt` binds the launcher SHA-256 (recomputed independently), the protocol-pinned Claude executable and digest-pinned container, the exact canonical create/start/cleanup argv and effective container config, fd-0-only OAuth delivery, authenticated liveness and cross-host clock-skew evidence, verified container deletion, empty stderr, and an exact final `VERDICT: ACCEPT` line. The v12 (scp creates an absent target, then hash-verify/chmod 0400/fsync), v13 (scheduler status and validator bind the same canonical `ledger/requests/<mode>-fable-review.json`), and v15 (liveness, clock bound, shared receipt builders validated pre-signing) findings each have visible implementations and dedicated regression tests.

**6. One-shot launch/signing boundaries — addressed.** Every child launch requires an executor launch permit bound to request hash, authorization, protocol, and the exact executor process identity (PID, start ticks, boot ID), reserved with `O_EXCL`; source closure is re-verified inside the executor before token consumption; the consumed marker precedes `Popen`; the immutable unsigned draft is the only signable object; seal-attempt markers are ordinal-1; non-executor signing attempts without a final artifact close the action; the analysis lane is strictly one-shot with launch intent/evidence, namespace-distinctness proof, reentry rejection, and the sealed-memfd `--ro-bind-data` capability at the fixed path.

**7. Exact-stack determinism — addressed.** The CUDA preflight runs fresh servers per attempt with distinct sockets/inodes under the 107-byte-validated `/dev/shm/p/<campaign-id>` namespace (93/92 bytes), pinned interpreter/bubblewrap hashes, TF32 disabled, exact production evaluator argv, batch shapes and worker concurrency from the frozen protocol, and treats any per-game or replay-hash inequality as a terminal failure. The corrected attempt-directory contract (plain, empty, non-symlink only) has all four regression tests.

**Incident `33641f25` remediation — addressed.** The issuer adds `/proc/driver/nvidia` only to the fault-injection preflight; both nested controllers omit it (tested for all three names, including fault-injection's positive case). The executor's `validate_nested_controller_mount_contract` independently rejects any of the three controller entrypoints with that submount, inside `validate_authorization`, and is directly tested. The v2 analyzer rehearsal is a real end-to-end production-analyzer execution with the full 595-receipt synthetic chain, empty-stream, one-shot, key-deletion, and v1-evidence rejection contracts, all fail-closed with mutation tests.
